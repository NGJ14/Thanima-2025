About Thanima

"Onam isn’t just a date on the calendar it’s a heartbeat that connects every Malayali." Thanima is VIT’s annual Onam celebration -a vibrant tribute to Kerala’s timeless traditions and the unbreakable bond of the Malayali community. Since 2002, Thanima has been more than just a fest; it’s a legacy. From cultural performances and sports to celebrity guests and festive spirit, it brings alive the soul of Onam on campus. A celebration of roots, pride, and pure Malayali joy — this is Thanima.
